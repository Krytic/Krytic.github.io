Opening Quote

Like moons make swell and wane the nescient seas,
so too the sky-strewn gods ordain the tidal fates of mortal days.
And yet - a notion born in lonely hours - come ebb, come flow,
come all that is beyond the breadth of our dominion:
be a moon unto yourself.
Even the waves of fate can break upon the shores of will.

— Elminster Aumar

Closing Quote

Isa lei -- Nanoqu rarawa,
Miko sana vodo e na mataka,
Bau nonuma, na nodatau lasa,
Mai Suva, nonuma tikoga.
Isa Lei. The purple shadows fall,
Sad the morrow will dawn upon my sorrow.
Ah, forget not when you are far away
Precious moments beside dear Suva Bay.

— Isa Lei, "The Fijian Farewell Song"
