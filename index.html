Physics

As a Physics student, I have a passion for Cosmology and looking at the early universe. I think that the two most fundamental questions we can ask ourselves as a species, are "where did we come from?" and "where are we going?". Cosmology looks at these questions and more to delve into the mysteries behind the Big Bang, and examines large-scale structures of the Universe, such as the supermassive black hole at the center of our Galaxy.
